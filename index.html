About Neon

Neon is a global payments and e-commerce platform built to give game publishers more revenue and independence from app stores. We believe commerce should be open and transparent—clear data, aligned incentives, and actionable insights.

Founded by veterans of payments, fintech, and gaming, Neon is replacing black-box incumbents with modern, developer-first infrastructure. We share the playbook, co-pilot key decisions, and take on the risk and complexity so our partners can focus on players.

Why join

As an early team member you’ll shape both the product and the culture. You’ll work alongside colleagues from Apple, Affirm, Unity, Visa and others, backed by investors including Thrive, a16z, Griffin Gaming, Ribbit, and Renegade. Your work will have direct impact on Neon’s growth and on the success of game creators worldwide.

If you want to build disruptive technology, challenge the status quo, and help creators thrive on their own terms—we’d like to talk.
